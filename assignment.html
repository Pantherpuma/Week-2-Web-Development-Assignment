Kaba University

Kaba University stands as one of Africa’s premier private institutions of higher learning. Established in 2025, the University quickly distinguished itself, earning recognition as the leading private university across Eastern, Central, and Southern Africa in the same year. Committed to academic excellence with a strong emphasis on innovation, Kaba University has become a hub for cutting-edge research and transformative learning. Notably, through its innovation-driven approach, the University has contributed to the development of one of the world’s most advanced AI models, ChatGPT.
